About me

I'm a PhD candidate in Economics at Harvard University. My research focuses on urban economics, industrial organization, and public economics, with a particular interest in transportation and infrastructure. I previously worked as a Data Scientist at Uber and earned dual degrees in Economics and Mathematics from the University of Chicago.
